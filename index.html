About Us

Who We Are

Ermira Xhunga Studio is specialized in architectural projects, covering every process: urban planning, architectural and construction project, bill of quantity and interior design. Based in Tirana, founded in 1994 in Durrës by Ermira Xhunga. Our work is guided by passion and work ethics to provide remarkable projects.

Why Choose Us

The trust that many clients have shown over the years in our professionalism is one of our strongest points, knowing that the relationship with the client is the most important thing at the end of every project, and not only that. Our staff tries to find a long-term or permanent solution in every situation and project it undertakes to realize. We like to perform at our best, regardless of the circumstances.
